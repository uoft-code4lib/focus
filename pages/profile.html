Research Focus

Eric Fong is a professor of sociology at the University of Toronto. He is current Vice President of the Canadian Population Society and former President of the North American Chinese Sociologists Association. Fong widely publishes in the areas of race and ethnic residential patterns and immigration. One of his current projects, funded by the Social Sciences and Humanities Research Council, examines the immigrant businesses in small and large cities.
